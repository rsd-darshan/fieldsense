# Research roadmap

## Near term

- Consolidate canonical data paths and metadata cards.
- Add deterministic training/evaluation scripts with fixed seeds.
- Record baseline metrics and confusion matrices under `results/`.

## Mid term

- Add uncertainty quantification and confidence-aware rollup variants.
- Evaluate heuristic threshold sensitivity and ablations.
- Integrate weather and soil-lab context for richer field features.

## Longer term

- Multi-region external validation with agronomy collaborators.
- Compare heuristic rollup vs learned meta-models under interpretability constraints.
- Publish benchmark protocol and reproducibility checklist.
