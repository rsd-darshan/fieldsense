# Engine thresholds

- pH stress when `< 5.5` or `> 8.0`
- moisture stress when `< 25` (high) and `> 85` (moderate)
- aridity trend when rainfall `< 40` and humidity `< 45`
- Nitrogen–phosphorus–potassium (N–P–K) imbalance when ratio deviation exceeds configured tolerance
- risk tiers from aggregate score:
  - low: `>= 75`
  - medium: `50-74`
  - high: `< 50`
